About DMMA Group Ltd

Built on site management,
cost-efficiency & safety

DMMA Group Ltd is a construction, trading and supply chain, and mining support services company. We operate as a direct and practical partner, managing on-the-ground project execution and large-scale general supplies — including electrical, mechanical, and structural materials and equipment.

Built on a foundation of direct site management, cost-efficiency, and job-site safety, we handle contracts of all sizes. We eliminate the complexities of procurement and project delivery, ensuring reliable results from initial sourcing to final project handover.

“We don't just supply materials — we deliver certainty. From the first requisition to the final handover, we are your boots on the ground.”
