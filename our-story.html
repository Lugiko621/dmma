From site technician to industrial force

Our story

From humble, boots-on-the-ground beginnings to a structured enterprise — DMMA Group Ltd stands for field reliability and execution on demand.

The journey

From a single technician
to a consolidated corporate force

Our company's founder, D. M. S. Masingija, was a determined graduate, a practical builder, and a visionary. Fresh out of college in 2019, Masingija began his journey entirely alone, working directly on-site as a local technician.

Driven by a sharp eye for market needs, he quickly expanded into general supplies. However, the true turning point came when he took on the heavy responsibility of supervising a major water project. Confronting the raw challenges of that site firsthand inspired him to institutionalize his operations, leading to the formal establishment of the business as Saraphina Brothers Limited in 2023.

“As the volume and scope of work rapidly diversified into heavier industrial sectors, the enterprise officially transitioned into DMMA Group Limited — a consolidated corporate force engineered to tackle the rigid challenges of construction, large-scale general supplies, and specialized mining support.”

From those humble beginnings, DMMA Group Ltd has grown beyond a local contractor into a structured enterprise, where our name stands for field reliability and execution on demand. Today, that same founding spirit of facing site challenges head-on lives on at DMMA and in each of our core divisions.
